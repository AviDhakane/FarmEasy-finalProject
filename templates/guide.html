Soil Testing


We recommand to do soil testing before taking any decision of farming. A soil test lets the farmers understand what amount of nutrients are already present in the soil and how much extra is needed. It helps in bringing increased uniformity of nutrient availability across the fields for more uniform crop growth.
Denotes the number of fertilizers to be used:
Understanding the exact deficiency in the soil can help farmers take the required measures. Fertilizers are used to provide additional nutrients to the plants, to improve the yield of the crops, and for the greening of lawns. There are inorganic fertilizers like potassium and phosphorus which are very limited resources. Such fertilizers can be avoided if not needed by the soil. Which fertilizers to use, and in which amount can be decided on the basis of soil tests. It also prevents over-fertilization.
